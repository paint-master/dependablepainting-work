Your Questions, Answered

We’re not just painters — we’re business owners like you, and we know how important it is to work with a contractor who’s reliable, clear, and committed to delivering real results. Here are answers to some of the most common questions we get about how we run things.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Dependable Painting is fully licensed and insured in the state of Alabama, including general liability and workers’ comp coverage for peace of mind.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes — you can request an estimate online or by phone. We aim to respond within 24–48 hours, and larger jobs may include an in-person walk-through.

Do you require a deposit?

We typically require a 25% deposit to secure your project on our schedule. Larger commercial projects may use milestone billing.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Baldwin and Mobile Counties, focusing on cities like Fairhope, Daphne, Spanish Fort, Loxley, and Bay Minette. We avoid Gulf Shores and Orange Beach unless it’s a large commercial job.

How do I pay?

We accept major credit cards, checks, ACH, and can invoice businesses. You'll always get digital documentation for your records.

Do you subcontract any work?

No. All work is performed by our trained in-house crew. No subs. No shortcuts.
