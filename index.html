Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes. We offer 100% free, no-pressure estimates. We'll walk your project, review scope, and provide a clear quote within 24–48 hours.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. We're fully licensed and insured for residential and commercial work in Alabama. Certificates are available upon request.

What kind of paint do you use?

We primarily use Sherwin-Williams products unless otherwise requested. For cabinets and interiors, we use scuff-resistant and washable formulas for high traffic durability.

How soon can you start?

Most jobs can begin within 1–2 weeks of your quote approval. We also offer rush scheduling when availability allows.

What’s your prep process?

We protect all floors, furniture, and fixtures. Surfaces are cleaned, sanded, patched, and primed as needed before applying any paint. Prep is 80% of the job and we don’t cut corners.

How do I book a project?

Easy—just head to our Contact page and fill out the form, or call us directly. We’ll get back to you quickly to schedule your estimate and lock in your timeline.
