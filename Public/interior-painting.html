Interior Painting: Your Questions Answered

How do you protect my furniture and floors?

We meticulously cover all furniture, floors, and non-painted surfaces with drop cloths and plastic sheeting. Your home will be treated with the utmost care.

What kind of paint do you use for interiors?

We primarily use premium, low-VOC (Volatile Organic Compound) paints from Sherwin-Williams, ensuring a durable finish and minimal odor for your family's comfort.

How long does interior paint take to dry?

Most interior paints are dry to the touch within 1-4 hours, and ready for a second coat in 2-4 hours. Full cure time can take several days to a few weeks.
