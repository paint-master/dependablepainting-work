Frequently Asked Questions

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Dependable Painting is fully licensed and insured in the state of Alabama, including general liability and workers’ comp coverage for peace of mind.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes — you can request an estimate online or by phone. We aim to respond within 24–48 hours, and larger jobs may include an in-person walk-through.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Baldwin and Mobile Counties, focusing on cities like Fairhope, Daphne, Spanish Fort, Loxley, and Bay Minette. We avoid Gulf Shores and Orange Beach unless it’s a large commercial job.

Do you subcontract any work?

No. All work is performed by our trained in-house crew. No subs. No shortcuts.
