Commercial Painting FAQs

How do you work around business hours?

We prioritize your business continuity. We offer flexible scheduling, including nights, weekends, and holiday periods, to minimize any disruption to your customers and staff.

Are you licensed and insured for commercial jobs?

Yes, absolutely. Dependable Painting is fully licensed and carries comprehensive general liability and workers' compensation insurance for all commercial projects.

What types of commercial properties do you service?

We service a wide range of properties, including retail stores, offices, medical facilities, restaurants, warehouses, and new construction projects.

What kind of paint do you use for commercial projects?

We use premium, low-VOC and zero-VOC commercial-grade paints from Sherwin-Williams and other top brands, selected for their durability, scuff-resistance, and professional finish.
